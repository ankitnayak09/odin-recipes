Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Paneer butter masala is a rich and creamy dish of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a tomato, butter, and cashew sauce. It is one of the most popular dishes in Indian restaurants and is a staple in many Indian households. The dish is usually served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 200g of paneer
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of cashews
  • 1/2 cup of cream
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon of red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they are golden brown
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cashews to the pan
  4. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the cashews are tender
  5. Let the mixture cool and then blend it into a smooth paste
  6. Heat the remaining butter in a pan
  7. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant
  8. Add the red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala to the pan
  9. Cook for a minute and then add the tomato-cashew paste
  10. Add sugar and salt to taste
  11. Add the paneer cubes and cream to the pan
  12. Cook for a few minutes until the paneer is heated through
  13. Serve hot with naan or rice
  14. Enjoy!
